DISTRICTS

This 244-hectare township project has been divided into districts. All districts bear the four pillars of Filinvest City but each one with its distinctive district character.

NORTHGATE

DISTRICT

Located next to Alabang-Zapote Road, this district houses the 18.7 hectares, Northgate Cyberzone, the Information Technology Park within Filinvest City.

WESTGATE

DISTRICT

Filinvest City's high-end, and master-planned retail and lifestyle center, that is home to popular dining establishments, car showrooms, residential havens, urban home essential shops, and wellness centers.

PALMS DISTRICT

Situated at the highest point of the city and is home to the Palms Country Club, an exclusive membership club and to the two most prestigious addresses in the in the city, Palms Pointe and Botanika Nature Residences.

WOODS DISTRICT

Situated at the highest point of the city and is home to the Palms Country Club, an exclusive membership club and to the two most prestigious addresses in the in the city, Palms Pointe and Botanika Nature Residences.

SOUTH STATION

DISTRICT

The development's main transportation hub, South Station serves as a point of convergence for public utility vehicles playing routes to and from Filinvest City.

CIVIC PLAZA

DISTRICT

This district serves as host to a variety of key public services and institutional facilities, such as community fire station, police station, public and private healthcare facilities and government offices.
